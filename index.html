Featured Project

The Language of Trauma and Hope: A Visual Analysis of Wonder Egg Priority

This project is a deep-dive analysis of how the anime Wonder Egg Priority uses its animation as a fundamental storytelling tool to translate complex psychological themes into a visceral visual experience.

Key Analysis Points:

  • Color & Mood: Deconstructed how the cool, purple-and-blue palette of the dream world creates a sense of beautiful isolation, contrasting with the desaturated tones of reality to highlight emotional pain.
  • Camera & Perspective: Analyzed how low-angle shots make the world feel imposing and how Dutch angles during monster encounters create disorientation and terror.
  • Animation & Emotion: Broke down how character movements shift from fluid to jerky and distorted to manifest abstract concepts like trauma and fear physically.

Conclusion: This analysis taught me that true storytelling is holistic. The "editing" and "visuals" are not separate from the script; they are the script. Every frame is an intentional choice that guides the audience's emotion.
